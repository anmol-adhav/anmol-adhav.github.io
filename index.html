Anmol Adhav

Postdoc | PhD | Structural Biology | Drug Design | Enzyme Characterization | Biochemistry

Curiosity drives my journey in science and innovation. My academic path began with a deep dive into structural biology, where I conducted protein crystallography experiments during my M.Sc followed by PhD. This experience ignited my fascination for the molecular underpinnings of biology and motivated my exploration into drug discovery, from hit identification to lead optimization.

Currently, as a postdoctoral researcher, I am focused on unraveling the mechanisms of bacterial defense systems. My work not only sheds light on fundamental biology but also aims to uncover and develop novel biotechnological tools inspired by nature's ingenuity.

I am passionate about translating scientific discovery into impactful solutions. Whether it's through collaborative research or innovation-driven projects, I am dedicated to expanding the boundaries of what's possible in biotech and contributing to the next generation of scientific advancements.
